About Graphic Design

Graphic design is a craft where professionals create visual content to communicate messages. By applying visual hierarchy and page layout techniques, designers use typography and picturesto meet users' specific needs and focus on the logic of displaying elements in interactive designs, to optimize the user experience.

CANDIDACY

In the sophomore year of study, students submit portfolios for entrance to the Graphic Design Concentration. Enrollment in 3000 and 4000 level graphic design courses is based upon this review.
